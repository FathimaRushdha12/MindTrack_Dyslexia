A Multimodal based Mobile Solution

for Early Dyslexia Detection:

Combining Handwriting, Eye Tracking and Reading Analysis (Mindtrack)

Know More

Our Purpose of Research

Dyslexia known as a condition of neurodevelopmental origin that mainly affects the ease with which a person reads, writes, and spells, typically recognized as a specific learning disorder in children. Individuals with dyslexia often have normal intelligence but struggle with processing language. Early detection through tools like handwriting analysis, reading behaviour and Eye tracking can help offer support at the right time.

This application is designed to help identify early signs of dyslexia using AI-based models that analyze user input through interactive tasks.
